Hi! I am currently a master student at Stanford University majoring in Robotics, advised by Prof. Jiajun Wu and Prof. Fei-Fei Li. I'm also working with Dr. Ruohan Gao and Dr. Yunzhu Li on multiple research projects. I received my B.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Purdue University in Mechanical Engineering. At Purdue University, I have been lucky to work with Prof. Karthik Ramani on Human-Computer Interaction Research.

My current research interests mainly focus on Multisensory Robotics, Robot Learning and Embodied AI. I am passionate about making sense of multisensory information to help robot in various manipulation tasks and solve real-world robotic problems. 

I am graduating in 2023 and looking for a PhD position. Please let me know if you are interested in my research experience.
